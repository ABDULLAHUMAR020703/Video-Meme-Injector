Sample Memes Folder

This folder is for storing your meme images that will be inserted into videos.

Supported image formats:
- JPG/JPEG
- PNG
- BMP
- GIF
- TIFF

Simply place your meme images in this folder, and the Video Memer application will randomly select from them when replacing black frames in your videos.

Tips:
- Use images with similar aspect ratios for best results
- Keep file sizes reasonable (under 5MB each)
- The app will automatically resize images to match your video dimensions
